Getting Here

Sylvester is easy to reach from every direction. Here are the most common routes.

From Albany

Take Hwy 82 East into Sylvester.

From I-75 Tifton

Take Exit 62 West into Sylvester.

From I-75 Cordele

Take Exit 99 West to Hwy 33, then head south into Sylvester.

From I-75 Ashburn

Take Exit 82 West into Sylvester.

From Moultrie

Take Hwy 33 North into Sylvester.
